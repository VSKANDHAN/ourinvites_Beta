4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following circumstances:

Eligible for Refund:

  • Technical Issues: If our platform fails to deliver the promised features
  • Payment Errors: Duplicate charges or unauthorized transactions
  • Service Unavailability: If we cannot provide the purchased service
  • Account Issues: Problems with account creation preventing service access

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Change of mind after successful service delivery
  • User error in invitation creation or customization
  • Dissatisfaction with design choices made by the user
  • Event cancellation or postponement
  • Requests made more than 7 days after purchase

5. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team within 7 days of purchase
  2. Provide your order details and reason for the refund request
  3. Include any relevant screenshots or documentation
  4. Allow up to 5 business days for our team to review your request
  5. Receive notification of approval or denial of your refund request

6. Refund Processing

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed
  • Bank processing times may vary (3-7 business days typically)
  • Access to premium features will be revoked upon refund processing

7. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer partial refunds:

  • When only some features are affected by technical issues
  • For service disruptions that impact but don't prevent usage
  • As a goodwill gesture for minor service inconveniences
